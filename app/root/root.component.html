Scandipedia.com is an information based database pertaining to mid-century furniture design. This website is an extensive encyclopedia that focuses on 20th Century Furniture designed for the Mid-Century Modern Furniture enthusiast, interior designer, dealer, picker, collector and anyone else who shares our passion for this timeless well-made design.  Scandipedia.com is a one of a kind source which provides the most accurate information of MCM designers, manufactures and articles on the Internet today.
